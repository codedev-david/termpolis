# Privacy Policy

**Termpolis**
Last updated: March 28, 2026

## Overview

Termpolis is a desktop terminal management application that runs entirely on your local machine. Your privacy is important to us, and Termpolis is designed to keep your data local.

## Data Collection

Termpolis does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Specifically:

- **No telemetry** — Termpolis does not send usage statistics, analytics, or crash reports to any remote server.
- **No account required** — Termpolis does not require you to create an account or sign in to use the application.
- **No tracking** — Termpolis does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, or any form of user tracking.

## Data Stored Locally

Termpolis stores the following data locally on your machine to provide its functionality:

- **Session data** — terminal configurations, workspace layouts, and user preferences are saved to your local filesystem so they persist between sessions.
- **Terminal history** — command history and terminal output remain in memory during your session and are not persisted to disk unless your shell is configured to do so independently.
- **AI agent configurations** — saved AI profiles, prompt templates, and swarm task descriptions are stored locally.

All locally stored data resides in your user profile directory and is never transmitted externally by Termpolis.

## Third-Party Services

Termpolis integrates with third-party AI tools (such as Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, Gemini CLI, and Aider) that you choose to install and run independently. These tools have their own privacy policies and may communicate with their respective cloud services. Termpolis does not control or intercept these communications — it simply provides a terminal environment in which these tools run.

Any data exchanged between AI tools and their cloud services is governed by the respective provider's privacy policy:
- [Anthropic (Claude)](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [OpenAI (Codex)](https://openai.com/privacy)
- [Google (Gemini)](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Error Reporting

Termpolis does not automatically send error reports. If you choose to report a bug via GitHub Issues, any information you share is voluntary and governed by GitHub's privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy

Termpolis is a developer tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted in the application's repository at [github.com/codedev-david/termpolis](https://github.com/codedev-david/termpolis).
